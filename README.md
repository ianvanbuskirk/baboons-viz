# Visualizing Baboon Foraging Patterns and Leadership Dynamics

An analysis of GPS tracking data from a troop of olive baboons at the Mpala Research Center, Kenya (Summer 2012). The work develops a custom coarse-graining scheme for discrete multi-scale analysis of movement trajectories and examines the dynamics of troop foraging decisions and leadership.

- **[Summary](summary/summary.pdf)** — writeup covering the dataset, coarse-graining method, and analysis of movement initiation and foraging direction
- **[Analysis](analysis/)** — notebooks generating the figures in the summary (`summary_figures.ipynb`) and exploring the coarse-graining scheme (`visualize_coarse_graining.ipynb`)
- **[Interactive Visualization](https://ianvanbuskirk.github.io/baboons-viz/)** — explore coarse-grained baboon movements day by day across spatial and temporal scales
